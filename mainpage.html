Mini-Matt is a polyester fabric with multiple uses. Lightweight, simple polyester weave that dries quickly. It is frequently used for dresses, skirts, aprons, and hospitality uniforms.

Any outfit made out of viscose proves to be a great option for summer. This is a result of the fabric's exceptionally breathable composition.
